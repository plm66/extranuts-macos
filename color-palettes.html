üß† Understanding Color Psychology in Note-Taking

Why color matters for productivity: Different colors trigger different mental states. Blue promotes focus and calm thinking (perfect for personal notes), while amber suggests energy and attention (ideal for work tasks). Pink stimulates creativity (great for ideas), and violet indicates imagination and innovation (perfect for projects).

Dark theme considerations: In dark interfaces, bright colors can cause eye strain. We use desaturated versions with transparency to maintain visual hierarchy while keeping the interface comfortable for extended use.

‚ôø Accessibility & Daltonism Considerations

Why we avoid green and brown: These colors are the most difficult to distinguish for people with color vision differences (deuteranopia and protanopia). By avoiding them, we ensure the interface works for everyone.
